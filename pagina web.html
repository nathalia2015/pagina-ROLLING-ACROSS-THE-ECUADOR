Traditions food of the regions of Ecuador

In Ecuador has four different regions which are Costa, Sierra, Amazonia and insular region, each one of them has its different gastronomic traditions, in which you can delight in the Sierra region you can find variety of typical dishes how we can describe the chicken broth, broth of 31 chucaro, mote pillo, hornado, the one of love and cascarita etc. In the coastal region it has its traditional gastronomy such as encebolla, bolon, banderas, ceviche breaded shrimp, etc. In the Amazonian region we have the typical dishes that are the chontacuro, the maíto, the inchicapi, the guanta and in the insular region with its typical dish rondón, crab soup, snail balls, beans with cocacola in pork, crab empanadas, cod with dad and yucca bread. But not all regions are the same in their gastronomic traditions.

the Aborigine Tribes in Ecuador Ecuador

Ecuador is a multicultural country with a predominantly mestizo population (82.2%), it has among its population nationalities and indigenous peoples, some of whom even have their own language. Its presence is greater in the Amazon region, even with a cross-border presence, and in the sierra, it is identified as the "set of previous and constitutive millennial peoples of the Ecuadorian State, who define themselves as such, who have a historical identity, language, and common culture, who live in a given territory through their institutions and traditional forms of social, economic, legal, political organization and the exercise of authority.
